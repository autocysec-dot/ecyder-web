Products

Security tooling for embedded systems

Four focused products that take teams from risk identification to continuous compliance evidence — built for automotive-grade engineering workflows.

01 / Products

Risk Assessment Tools

Automate Threat Analysis and Risk Assessment (TARA) across your vehicle architecture. eCyder's Risk Assessment Tools turn ISO/SAE 21434 threat modeling into a repeatable, auditable workflow instead of a one-off spreadsheet exercise.

  • Asset, threat and attack path modeling mapped to ISO/SAE 21434 Clause 15
  • Automated impact and attack feasibility scoring
  • Risk treatment tracking with sign-off history
  • Exportable risk registers for audits and homologation
ISO/SAE 21434 TARA Automotive
02 / Products

Vulnerability Management Tools

Track vulnerabilities across ECUs, firmware and third-party/supplier software in one place. Correlate CVEs against your components automatically and keep a continuous view of exposure across the product lifecycle.

  • Continuous CVE and advisory monitoring against your component inventory
  • Severity scoring aligned to CVSS and automotive risk context
  • Patch and remediation workflow with supplier tracking
  • Reporting built for CSMS post-development monitoring obligations
CVE Monitoring CSMS Patch Management
03 / Products

SBOM Generator Tool

Generate accurate, standards-compliant Software Bills of Materials for embedded firmware and supplier deliverables — a foundational requirement for modern automotive cybersecurity and software update management.

  • CycloneDX and SPDX export formats
  • Component, license and known-vulnerability mapping
  • Works across firmware images and source-level builds
  • Supports SUMS (ISO 24089) and supply-chain transparency requirements
SBOM CycloneDX SPDX Supply Chain
04 / Products

PSoE Tool

The PSoE Tool centralizes Product Security of Evidence — mapping the cybersecurity activities your teams already perform to the work products auditors expect, so certification readiness stops being a scramble.

  • Work-product mapping to ISO/SAE 21434 clauses
  • Evidence collection linked directly to TARA and CSMS activities
  • Audit-ready reporting for internal and third-party assessors
  • Single source of truth across program, project and post-development phases
Audit Evidence ISO/SAE 21434 Compliance
