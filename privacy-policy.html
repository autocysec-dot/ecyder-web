Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

eCyder ("we", "us", "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect through ecyder.com, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Cookies

This site uses cookies to deliver its services and to analyze traffic. Information about your use of this site may be shared with analytics providers, including Google. By using this site, you agree to its use of cookies. See Google's cookie policy for more information.

How we use information

  • To respond to inquiries sent to info@ecyder.com.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited data with service providers (such as analytics or hosting providers) strictly to operate this site.

Your choices

You can decline non-essential cookies via the cookie banner shown on this site, or by adjusting your browser settings. To request access to, correction of, or deletion of information you've shared with us, email info@ecyder.com.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to info@ecyder.com.
